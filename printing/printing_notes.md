## Settings
(Minimum 10x10x10 Inch 3D Print build Area)
- Material: PLA
- Nozzle Temperature: 190-220 Degrees Celcius
- Bed Temperature: 50-60 Degrees Celcius
- Print Speed: 40-60 mm/s
- Layer height: 0.1-0.3mm
- Infill: 20-50%
- Supports: 10-15%
